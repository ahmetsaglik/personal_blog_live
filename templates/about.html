About Me

My name is Ahmet Sağlık and I am 25 years old. I graduated from Necmettin Erbakan University Computer Engineering Department in 2020. I spent most of my education life dealing with the Python Programming Language. During my education, I completed the first of the compulsory internships in the IT department of Kipaş Holding. I did my second internship at Tekrom Teknoloji A.Ş. in the R&D department. During the internships, I learned a lot about machine learning. I am interested in machine learning, deep learning, image processing and data analysis. I have done various projects related to these fields. Some of these are “Percent Instant Emotion Estimation”, “Voice Assis-tant”, “Electricity Bill Estimation System with Regression Algorithms”, “Emotion Analysis with Natural Language Processing”, “Language Prediction System”, “Movie Recommendation System”. In addition to these, I was able to compete as a finalist in the Artificial Intelligence in Health competition held within TEKNOFEST. I also do backend development with Django Framework. I publish the sites I make on Heroku for reference. I have been serving through various freelancer sites for the last year.
